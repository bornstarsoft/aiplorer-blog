In a rapidly evolving digital landscape, Z3Soft is setting a new standard in cybersecurity with its innovative Agentic Security Platform (ASP). This next-generation solution integrates advanced AI technology with traditional security measures, shifting the paradigm from passive defense to proactive threat detection and response. CEO Park Ju-seon emphasizes that the future of security lies not just in capability but in a comprehensive approach that combines various tools into a unified platform.

Z3Soft's strategy targets small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) by offering a subscription-based model that lowers entry barriers to advanced security solutions. By focusing on international markets like Japan and the U.S. first, Z3Soft aims to establish a successful business model that can later be adapted for the domestic market. As the demand for integrated security solutions grows, will Z3Soft's innovative approach reshape the cybersecurity landscape for SMEs?
