SciSpace is a research and learning tool that may help users read papers, organize research notes, and prepare source-checking workflows. It can be useful when a person wants support for understanding research material before reviewing the original paper in detail.

Beginners may use SciSpace-style workflows to prepare questions, organize literature-review notes, or draft explanations for human review. Any AI-generated summary, paper explanation, citation, or conclusion should be checked against the original source before it is used for school, research, professional writing, or public publishing.
