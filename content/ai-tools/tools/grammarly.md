## Overview

Grammarly is a writing and communication assistant. It can be useful for people who want support with drafting, rewriting, clarity, tone, grammar support, and everyday communication.

Grammarly's plans, AI assistance, writing suggestions, access rules, and policies may change. Users should check the official Grammarly website, support center, pricing information, and privacy or security pages before relying on a specific feature.

## Useful For

Beginners may use Grammarly to review rough emails, documents, messages, or other drafts before sharing them. It can help users think through clarity, tone, style, and wording options, but suggestions should be checked before accepting them.

Writing feedback should be treated as a draft aid, not a replacement for human judgment. Users should make sure the final text is accurate, appropriate for the audience, and suitable for the context.

## Notes

Features, plans, AI assistance, grammar-checking behavior, plagiarism-related features, enterprise or team availability, integrations, browser extension availability, app availability, model access, and regional availability may change. Check official Grammarly pages for current details before relying on a specific feature.

Do not enter sensitive or confidential text unless you understand the relevant privacy, workplace, school, organization, and data handling policies. Grammarly suggestions should be treated as writing assistance, not as a guarantee that text is correct, appropriate, current, private, or safe to use without review.
