## Overview

ChatGPT is an AI assistant from OpenAI. It can be useful for people who want help drafting, rewriting, brainstorming, summarizing, learning, coding, or working through everyday productivity tasks.

## Useful For

Beginners may use ChatGPT to create a first draft, ask for simpler explanations, organize rough notes, or explore different ways to approach a task. It can also support coding practice and troubleshooting, but important outputs should be checked carefully before use.

## Notes

Features, pricing, model access, and availability may change. Check the official ChatGPT website and pricing page for current details before choosing a plan or relying on a specific feature.
