SlidesAI is an AI presentation drafting tool that can help users turn notes, topics, or rough text into slide deck drafts. It can be useful when a person wants to prepare an early presentation outline, organize written ideas, or create a first pass before final review.

## Useful For

Beginners may use SlidesAI to think through slide structure, turn rough notes into a draft, prepare a lesson or report outline, or explore an early deck direction. Any generated slides should be reviewed carefully before they are shared, published, or used in a real business, school, client, or public setting.

Presentation tools should be treated as drafting support, not as a guarantee that facts are correct, sources are complete, images are licensed, brand rules are followed, or the output is ready to publish.
