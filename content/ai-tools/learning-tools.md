AI learning tools can help with research questions, source review, summaries, study notes, and learning workflows. This category is for students, researchers, lifelong learners, and people who need help organizing information before reading or writing in more depth.

These tools should be treated as support for reading and thinking, not as final authorities. Verify original sources, review citations and summaries carefully, and take extra care with academic, legal, medical, financial, confidential, or sensitive documents.
