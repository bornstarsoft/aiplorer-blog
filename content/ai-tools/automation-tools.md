Automation tools can help connect apps, reduce repetitive manual work, and support repeatable productivity workflows. This category is for users who want to plan, test, and manage app-to-app workflows with careful review.

Automation can affect connected accounts, customer data, business data, and operational workflows. Test automations before relying on them, review app permissions carefully, and check each official website for current pricing, limits, security, and availability details.
